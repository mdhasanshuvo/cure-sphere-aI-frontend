# ⚡ QUICK FIX: HOW TO ACCESS AI DOCTOR

## 🚀 In 3 Steps:

### **Step 1: Open Patient Dashboard**
```
Login with your Patient credentials
   ↓
You see a navigation menu
   ↓
Click: "Patient Dashboard" (or "Dashboard")
```

### **Step 2: Look RIGHT**
```
Patient Dashboard opens
   ↓
Look at the RIGHT SIDE of the page
   ↓
You'll see a BLUE BOX
```

### **Step 3: Click Button**
```
In the blue box, you'll see:
"AI Doctor Available 24/7"
"Get instant health guidance..."

[Start Consultation] ← CLICK THIS BUTTON

A white button on blue background
```

---

## ✅ That's It!

The AI Chat will now open. Start typing your symptoms!
